No Bake Cookies

Grandma's Favorite No Bake Cookies.

These cookies were always my favorite to make when growing up. It is an easy recipe my Grandma taught me to make when I was very little. They are always a favorite when brought to family dinners (if you don't eat all of them before dinner time!).

Ingredients

  • Sugar (2 cups)
  • Butter (1/2 cup)
  • Cocoa Powder (3 tbs)
  • Salt (1/2 tsp)
  • Milk (1/2 cup)
  • Peanut Butter (1/2 cup)
  • Vanilla (1 tsp)
  • Quick Oats (3 cups)

Steps

  1. Lay wax paper over a dish towel across counter to place cookies on when done.
  2. In a large pot mix together sugar, butter, cocoa powder, salt, and milk.
  3. Bring to a boil and let boil for 1 minute.
  4. Remove from heat.
  5. Beat in peanut butter and vanilla.
  6. Stir in oats.
  7. Drop on waxed paper by the spoonful.
