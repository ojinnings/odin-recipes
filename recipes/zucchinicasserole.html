Zucchini Casserole

Easy Summer Dinner

Summer is the best time to get fresh zucchini from the garden! This recipe makes it easy to use it up in a delicious way. This makes a great dinner or a sidedish to bring to family get togethers.

Ingredients

  • Sausage (1 lb)
  • Stove Top Stuffing (1 pkg)
  • Condensed Soup (1 can)
  • Sour Cream (1 cup)
  • Zucchini (4-5 cups chopped)
  • Butter
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Brown the sausage.
  2. While that's going, put 8 oz boiling water in the stuffing mix with a little butter and stir it up. Set aside.
  3. When sausage is done, add zucchini and stir fry it for 5 minutes. Add seasoning as desired. DO NOT cook until done or it will be mush after you bake it.
  4. Mix in soup and sour cream.
  5. Sprinkle stuffing on top.
  6. Drizzle melted butter over the stuffing.
  7. Cover pan and put it in the oven at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
  8. Remove lid/foil and bake another 20 minutes.
  9. Remove from oven and enjoy!
